        About DisasterVision 3D

        DisasterVision 3D is an immersive educational platform designed to bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical disaster response.

        Our Mission

        To reduce disaster-related casualties by providing realistic, risk-free environments where users can practice life-saving maneuvers.

        The Technology

        Utilizing high-fidelity 3D graphics and interactive physics, we simulate real-world scenarios like earthquakes and floods.

        Our Team

        We are a group of students dedicated to leveraging technology for social safety and community awareness.
